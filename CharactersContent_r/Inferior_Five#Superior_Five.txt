bunny superstrength terrible grades the inferior five or i5 are a parody superhero team appearing in books by the american publisher dc comics created by writer e nelson bridwell and artist joe orlando the team premiered in the dc comics title showcase 62 may june 1966 1 the premise is that the characters are the children of members of a superhero team called the freedom brigade a parody of the justice league of america 2 in their early appearances the team went up against spoofs of the marvel comics heroes showcase 63 featured brute brainard who was exposed to phi beta kappa radiation and became the giant green hulk known as man mountain and in 65 the team visited dean egghead s superhero academy to meet the five young egg s men 3 when the team got their own series early issues
