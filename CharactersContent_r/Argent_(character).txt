abilities edit argent can generate solid energy constructs out of silver plasma colored bright red in the animated series in simple forms such as protective shields daggers and battering rams while she initially could not fly she could create slides and flying platforms to carry herself and others through the air her silver plasma energy is said to be of a similar wavelength to the green plasma energies of a green lantern s ring in jla rock of ages argent s powers in the future had evolved to become almost identical to a green lantern s as she was able to fly and could create more complex energy constructs such as an entire army of silver plasma creatures and make them act on their own accord but remain under her control in other media edit television edit argent appeared in the teen
