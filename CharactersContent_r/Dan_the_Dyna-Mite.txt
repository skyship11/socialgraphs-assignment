possessed a ring which controlled his powers releasing them only when the two rings came into contact originally he wore only one of the rings which gave him enhanced strength speed and resistance to injury and energy generation but after his mentor died he discovered he could wear both rings and release the power by pressing both rings together himself reception edit american comic book
