first appeared in justice league of america 10 1962 created by gardner fox and mike sekowsky he is depicted as an mystic sorcerer obsessed with restoring himself to his former might after being robbed of much of his power during a battle with doctor mist 1 while typically empowered by the demonic powers of a trio of brothers known as the demons three whose soul he sold to in a faustian deal the character also frequently targets other magical entities and objects to strengthen his power putting him frequently at odds with numerous superhero teams the character has appeared in live action in
