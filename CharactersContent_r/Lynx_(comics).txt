conflict with both robin and the huntress eventually she came to hate the idea that the ghost dragons were led by a white man and after king snake killed a member of the triads an act she considered stopped by robin and spoiler citation needed subsequently she spent time as a mercenary but eventually returned to gotham and resumed control of the ghost dragons when gotham was declared a no man s
