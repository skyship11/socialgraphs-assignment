previous claim of him being abandoned instead the story showed both of his parents alive his father randy as an abusive alcoholic and his mother perpetually using the phone and smoking due to his lack of parental involvement tad began to read comic books as a means of escapist fantasy 9 soon he began to resent his father who earned money through the sale of stolen goods believing firmly in the world of comic books tad performs an act he believes to be heroic the framing of his father tad informed his father s crack dealer of an employer and accused his father of being a snitch as a result his father was murdered and his mother sent to jail after being sent to live with foster parents he ran away and began to live on his own due to his lack of a job he was usually a vagrant and could afford to feed himself only through stealing from criminals following his capture at the hands of nightwing and subsequent arrest for the murder of cisco blaine he was revealed to have been left on a blüdhaven bus on christmas eve modus operandi edit nite wing saw himself as a hero and he sought to bring justice to the world but his methods were very flawed he had killed a number of people
