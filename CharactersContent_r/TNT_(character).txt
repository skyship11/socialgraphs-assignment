generation tnt is a dc comics superhero from the 1940s tnt and his side kick dan the dyna mite were created by mort weisinger for dc comics and made their debut in star spangled comics 7 april 1942 1 the human hand grenades had a short lived career during the golden age of comic books reappearing occasionally in reprint form during the 1970s returning in super friends 12 and appearing from time to
